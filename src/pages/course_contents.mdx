# course_contents

Course content is the sub part of the course that contains the materials about the course. this is the part where users actually learn the course.

## The course content model

The course_content model contains all the information about the course_contents.

### Properties

<Properties>
  <Property name="_id" type="string">
    Unique identifier for the course_content.
  </Property>
  <Property name="course" type="object">
    The course information containing this content.
  </Property>
  <Property name="title" type="string">
    The title of course content.
  </Property>
  <Property name="description" type="string">
    The description or content material.
  </Property>
  <Property name="thumbnail" type="string">
    The thumbail of the course that will be displayed when its a reading material. Otherwise it will be null
  </Property>
  <Property name="source" type="string">
    A Url for participant's submission / supporting file.
  </Property>
  <Property name="assignment" type="object">
    Instruction for giving task / assignment for participants.
  </Property>
  <Property name="material" type="string">
    The link of the material file. could be video or pdf file.
  </Property>
    <Property name="duration" type="number">
    The duration of the video, if pdf will be 0.
  </Property>
  <Property name="type" type="string">
    The type of material
  </Property>
  <Property name="created_at" type="timestamp">
    Timestamp of when the course_content was created.
  </Property>
  <Property name="updated_at" type="timestamp">
    Timestamp of when the course_content was updated.
  </Property>
</Properties>

## The assignment model

The assignment model contains the information of the assignment

### Properties

<Properties>
  <Property name="_id" type="string">
    Unique identifier for the course_content.
  </Property>
  <Property name="title" type="string">
    The title of course content.
  </Property>
  <Property name="instruction" type="string">
    The instruction of the assignment
  </Property>
  <Property name="contentId" type="string">
    Referring to the content material of the course.
  </Property>
  <Property name="submissions" type="array">
    The student assignment's submission
  </Property>
  <Property name="posted" type="timestamp">
    Timestamp of when the assignment was created.
  </Property>
</Properties>
